From Wikipedia

The Cabot Performing Arts Center is located at 286 Cabot Street in Beverly, Massachusetts. History For more than ninety years the Cabot Street Cinema Theatre has been an important part of the Boston's North Shore community. Harris and Glover Ware, two brothers and former vaudeville musicians from Marblehead, Massachusetts, built the Cabot eight years after the construction of their first Beverly theater, the Larcom Theatre.
