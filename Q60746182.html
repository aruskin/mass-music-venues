From Wikipedia

The MGM Music Hall at Fenway is a 5,009-capacity music venue located to the direct northeast of Fenway Park in Boston, Massachusetts. Boston mayor Michelle Wu held the venue's ribbon-cutting ceremony on August 22, 2022, followed by a short private concert by Guster for the students of Tufts University. The venue's first public concert was performed by Massachusetts-bred rock band Godsmack on August 27. [...]
